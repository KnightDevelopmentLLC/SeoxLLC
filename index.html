THE STORY
ABOUT
SEOX

A Label Born From Fire and Vision

SEOX Records emerged from a singular conviction: that the world's most powerful voices deserved more than the music industry's assembly line. That artistry — true, raw, uncompromised artistry — required an infrastructure built with the same intensity it was born from.

Founded under SEOX, LLC, the label was forged as both a creative sanctuary and a business empire — a rare combination that allows artists to create without ceiling and succeed without compromise.

"We don't sign artists. We recognize kings, and we build kingdoms."

Every decision at SEOX is made with one question at its center: does this serve the art? That question governs everything — from A&R to marketing, from contract terms to creative direction.

MISSION

To elevate voices that define eras — building artists whose legacies outlive the culture that created them.

PHILOSOPHY

Art is not product. It is prophecy. We treat it accordingly — with the reverence of scholars and the aggression of warriors.

IDENTITY

Royal. Dark. Sovereign. We carry the aesthetic of those who have always known their power and never needed to prove it.

DEMO
SUBMISSIONS

The Gate to the Kingdom

SEOX Records receives submissions from artists across the globe — and we listen to every single one. Not because we have to, but because the next legend might be in the next inbox.

We are not looking for perfect. We are looking for undeniable. The rawness, the vision, the sound that makes you stop everything and listen again.

If you believe you have what it takes to carry the SEOX legacy — submit your work. The throne is earned, not given.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Submit your strongest 2–3 tracks. Quality over quantity — always.
Include a brief artist bio and your creative vision. We want to know who you are, not just what you sound like.
Provide streaming links (Spotify, SoundCloud, YouTube) or high-quality file uploads.
Be original. We have zero interest in imitation — we want to hear what only you can create.
Include your contact information and social media handles for verification.
All genres considered. The SEOX aesthetic transcends genre — it is a state of mind.
PLEASE NOTE Due to the volume of submissions we receive, SEOX Records responds only to artists we wish to pursue further conversations with. If you haven't heard back within 60 days, please feel free to resubmit with new material. We appreciate your patience and your trust in bringing your art to us.
