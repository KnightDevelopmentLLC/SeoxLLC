Our Story

BUILT FOR
TOMORROW

"We believe the next great sound doesn't come from the biggest studio or the richest label. It comes from a bedroom, a garage, a dream that hasn't been heard yet."

SEOX, LLC was founded on a simple principle: the music industry is broken, and the artists pay the price. We're here to change the equation. More creative control. More revenue. More freedom.

Who We Are

A Label Designed for the Digital Age

The music industry is transforming. Streaming is fragmenting. Artists need partners, not gatekeepers. SEOX was built from the ground up to operate in this new world — with the tools, technology, and vision to match.

Join Us
The Origin

How It Started

SEOX began in 2024 — not in a boardroom, but in the basement of a Brooklyn apartment. Frustrated by the gatekeeping and exploitation rampant in the industry, our founders decided to build the label they always wished existed. One that puts artists first, always.

The Mission

What Drives Us

We exist to find extraordinary music and give it the platform it deserves. We work with artists across every genre — hip-hop, electronic, alternative, experimental — unified by one thing: uncompromising creative vision. We don't chase trends. We set them.

The Future

Where We're Going

We're scaling globally. New artist signings, new studio partnerships, new territories. But our core will never change: make great music, support the people who make it, and build something that lasts. The future of sound starts with SEOX.
