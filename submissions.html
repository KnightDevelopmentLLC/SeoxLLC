FAQ

Common Questions

Do I keep my rights?

Yes. We structure every deal with artist ownership in mind. We believe in shared success, not ownership extraction. Specifics are discussed during the signing process.

How long does a response take?

We aim to respond within 2–4 weeks. Volume permitting. We're a small team that actually listens, so we'd rather take the time and give you a real response.

What genres do you accept?

All of them. Seriously. Our roster spans rap, electronic, alt-rock, ambient, experimental, and everything in between. The only requirement is that it's real.

Do I need to be established?

No. We've signed artists with zero followers and first-time producers. We look at potential, not metrics. Show us your vision.
